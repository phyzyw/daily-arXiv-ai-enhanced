### [1] [The Symmetric Perceptron: a Teacher-Student Scenario](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25440)
*Giovanni Catania, Aurélien Decelle, Suhanee Korpe*

Main category: cond-mat.dis-nn

TL;DR: 本文提出并解决了对称二元感知器的师生模型，将传统的存储模型转化为一个具有保证解的植入推理问题，并分析了不同势函数和温度下的相图。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统的感知器模型主要关注存储容量和学习动力学，而师生模型提供了一个将学习视为推理问题的自然框架。本文旨在研究对称感知器在师生设置下的学习行为，并提供一个全面的相图结构。

Method: 研究人员通过 annealed 和 quenched 的自由熵计算，在高维极限下分析了师生模型，并结合了 piecewise constant 和 linear 损失函数，使用 replica 方法和 replica-symmetric ansatz。

Result: 研究结果揭示了在样本密度、边界距离和温度三个控制参数下的相图结构，发现学习过程由二阶不稳定性驱动，产生与教师相关的次优状态，随后发生一阶跃迁到完全对齐。这种结构取决于势函数的选择以及次优解的亚稳性和熔化过程。

Conclusion: 本文对对称感知器的师生模型进行了全面的分析，揭示了其独特的学习景观，为理解感知器模型的学习动力学和优化算法提供了新的见解，并为基于蒙特卡洛的优化算法提供了参考。

Abstract: We introduce and solve a teacher-student formulation of the symmetric binary Perceptron, turning a traditionally storage-oriented model into a planted inference problem with a guaranteed solution at any sample density. We adapt the formulation of the symmetric Perceptron which traditionally considers either the u-shaped potential or the rectangular one, by including labels in both regions. With this formulation, we analyze both the Bayes-optimal regime at for noise-less examples and the effect of thermal noise under two different potential/classification rules. Using annealed and quenched free-entropy calculations in the high-dimensional limit, we map the phase diagram in the three control parameters, namely the sample density $α$, the distance between the origin and one of the symmetric hyperplanes $κ$ and temperature $T$, and identify a robust scenario where learning is organized by a second-order instability that creates teacher-correlated suboptimal states, followed by a first-order transition to full alignment. We show how this structure depends on the choice of potential, the interplay between metastability of the suboptimal solution and its melting towards the planted configuration, which is relevant for Monte Carlo-based optimization algorithms.

Main category: physics.chem-ph

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种结合可传递深度学习变分蒙特卡洛 (VMC) 和高斯过程回归的新框架，用于高效且准确地探索强相关系统的分子势能面 (PES)。该方法能够在化学相关几何分布中实现零点化学精度。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统计算化学方法在探索复杂分子势能面时面临挑战，尤其是在处理强相关系统、激发态过程和光-物质相互作用时。现有方法通常需要牺牲精度或效率，或者依赖于对特定区域定制的量子化学方法。

Method: 该方法通过持续采样核构型并在 VMC 优化电子波函数的同时，获得可传递的描述。使用高斯过程回归对 VMC 能量和力进行估计，并对噪声数据进行聚合，从而稀疏地评估势能面。这种方法结合了可传递深度学习 VMC 和高斯过程回归，以估计能量、力以及海森矩阵。

Result: 该方法能够准确高效地探索复杂的势能面景观，包括结构弛豫、过渡态搜索和最低能量路径，适用于基态和激发态系统。在化学相关几何分布中，该方法实现了零点化学精度。

Conclusion: 这项工作为研究具有显著多参考特性的系统中的键的断裂、形成和大的结构重排开辟了道路，并为更准确地模拟化学过程提供了新的可能性。

Abstract: A faithful description of chemical processes requires exploring extended regions of the molecular potential energy surface (PES), which remains challenging for strongly correlated systems. Transferable deep-learning variational Monte Carlo (VMC) offers a promising route by efficiently solving the electronic Schrödinger equation jointly across molecular geometries at consistently high accuracy, yet its stochastic nature renders direct exploration of molecular configuration space nontrivial. Here, we present a framework for highly accurate ab initio exploration of PESs that combines transferable deep-learning VMC with a cost-effective estimation of energies, forces, and Hessians. By continuously sampling nuclear configurations during VMC optimization of electronic wave functions, we obtain transferable descriptions that achieve zero-shot chemical accuracy within chemically relevant distributions of molecular geometries. Throughout the subsequent characterization of molecular configuration space, the PES is evaluated only sparsely, with local approximations constructed by estimating VMC energies and forces at sampled geometries and aggregating the resulting noisy data using Gaussian process regression. Our method enables accurate and efficient exploration of complex PES landscapes, including structure relaxation, transition-state searches, and minimum-energy pathways, for both ground and excited states. This opens the door to studying bond breaking, formation, and large structural rearrangements in systems with pronounced multi-reference character.

### [3] [Autotuning T-PaiNN: Enabling Data-Efficient GNN Interatomic Potential Development via Classical-to-Quantum Transfer Learning](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.24752)

Main category: physics.chem-ph

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种名为T-PaiNN的迁移学习框架，通过利用廉价的经典力场数据预训练，显著提高了图神经网络（GNN）原子间势能的训练数据效率。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统的基于图神经网络的原子间势能模型需要大量的昂贵量子力学训练数据，限制了其应用。因此，需要一种更有效的数据利用方法。

Method: T-PaiNN框架首先使用大规模的经典分子模拟数据预训练PaiNN原子间势能模型，然后使用较小规模的密度泛函理论（DFT）数据集进行微调（自适应调整）。

Result: 实验结果表明，T-PaiNN在QM9数据集和液态水模拟中均优于仅使用DFT数据训练的模型，在低数据条件下，误差降低可达25倍，训练收敛速度更快，并且能更准确地预测能量、力以及密度和扩散等实验相关性质。

Conclusion: 该研究表明，从经典力场进行迁移学习是一种实用且计算效率高的策略，可以开发出高精度、数据高效的GNN原子间势能，从而能够更广泛地应用于复杂的化学系统。

Abstract: Machine-learned interatomic potentials (MLIPs), particularly graph neural network (GNN)-based models, offer a promising route to achieving near-density functional theory (DFT) accuracy at significantly reduced computational cost. However, their practical deployment is often limited by the large volumes of expensive quantum mechanical training data required. In this work, we introduce a transfer learning framework, Transfer-PaiNN (T-PaiNN), that substantially improves the data efficiency of GNN-MLIPs by leveraging inexpensive classical force field data. The approach consists of pretraining a PaiNN MLIP architecture on large-scale datasets generated from classical molecular simulations, followed by fine-tuning (dubbed autotuning) using a comparatively small DFT dataset. We demonstrate the effectiveness of autotuning T-PaiNN on both gas-phase molecular systems (QM9 dataset) and condensed-phase liquid water. Across all cases, T-PaiNN significantly outperforms models trained solely on DFT data, achieving order-of-magnitude reductions in mean absolute error while accelerating training convergence. For example, using the QM9 data set, error reductions of up to 25 times are observed in low-data regimes, while liquid water simulations show improved predictions of energies, forces, and experimentally relevant properties such as density and diffusion. These gains arise from the model's ability to learn general features of the potential energy surface from extensive classical sampling, which are subsequently refined to quantum accuracy. Overall, this work establishes transfer learning from classical force fields as a practical and computationally efficient strategy for developing high-accuracy, data-efficient GNN interatomic potentials, enabling broader application of MLIPs to complex chemical systems.

### [4] [When Is Collective Intelligence a Lottery? Multi-Agent Scaling Laws for Memetic Drift in LLMs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.24676)
*Hidenori Tanaka*

Main category: cs.AI

TL;DR: 本文研究了大型语言模型（LLM）多智能体系统中集体智能的形成机制，揭示了在缺乏先验偏好时，群体如何通过“膜质漂移”（memetic drift）达到共识，并提出了相应的scaling laws。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 随着LLM多智能体系统在决策中的应用日益广泛，理解其结果是否反映集体推理、系统性偏见还是纯粹的随机性变得至关重要。现有研究表明，即使个体没有偏好，群体也能迅速达成共识，但其背后的机制尚不明确。

Method: 本文引入了“量化单纯形 gossip”（QSG）模型，模拟了LLM多智能体之间的交互过程，其中智能体通过采样彼此的输出进行学习。通过QSG模拟和LLM命名游戏实验，研究了群体规模、通信带宽、上下文适应率和智能体内部不确定性等因素对群体共识的影响。

Result: 研究发现，群体行为会经历从“漂移主导”到“选择主导”的转变。在漂移主导阶段，共识更像是一种彩票；而在选择主导阶段，微小的偏见会被放大并影响结果。研究还推导了漂移引起的极化现象的scaling laws，并在模拟和实验中得到了验证。

Conclusion: 本文提供了一个研究多智能体系统中社会表征形成集体机制的框架，揭示了群体共识的形成可能并非总是基于集体推理，而是受到随机漂移的影响。这些结果对于理解和控制LLM多智能体系统的行为具有重要意义。

Abstract: Multi-agent systems powered by large language models (LLMs) are increasingly deployed in settings that shape consequential decisions, both directly and indirectly. Yet it remains unclear whether their outcomes reflect collective reasoning, systematic bias, or mere chance. Recent work has sharpened this question with naming games, showing that even when no individual agent favors any label a priori, populations rapidly break symmetry and reach consensus. Here, we reveal the mechanism by introducing a minimal model, Quantized Simplex Gossip (QSG), and trace the microscopic origin of this agreement to mutual in-context learning. In QSG, agents maintain internal belief states but learn from one another's sampled outputs, so one agent's arbitrary choice becomes the next agent's evidence and can compound toward agreement. By analogy with neutral evolution, we call this sampling-driven regime memetic drift. QSG predicts a crossover from a drift-dominated regime, where consensus is effectively a lottery, to a selection regime, where weak biases are amplified and shape the outcome. We derive scaling laws for drift-induced polarization as a function of population size, communication bandwidth, in-context adaptation rate, and agents' internal uncertainty, and we validate them in both QSG simulations and naming-game experiments with LLM populations. Together, these results provide a framework for studying the collective mechanisms of social representation formation in multi-agent systems.

### [5] [Grokking as a Falsifiable Finite-Size Transition](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.24746)
*Yuda Bi, Chenyu Zhang, Qiheng Wang, Vince D Calhoun*

Main category: cs.LG

TL;DR: 本文将Grokking现象视为可检验的有限尺寸相变，并应用凝聚态物理的诊断方法，为该现象提供了可验证的框架。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有研究中，Grokking现象常被描述为“相变”，但缺乏可检验的有限尺寸输入。本文旨在通过提供这些输入，将Grokking的讨论从描述性转向诊断性。

Method: 本文将循环群Zp的群阶p作为可延展变量，并将保留的谱头尾对比作为表示层序参数。随后，应用凝聚态物理的诊断链，包括粗网格扫描和近临界审计，并使用Binder曲线交叉和易感性比较来分析。

Result: 研究结果表明，Binder曲线存在共享的有限尺寸边界，且易感性比较强烈否定了平滑交叉解释（∆AIC = 16.8）。

Conclusion: 本文为Grokking现象提供了一个可作为定量有限尺寸主张进行测试的框架，尽管相变顺序尚未确定。这为未来研究Grokking现象提供了更严格的检验标准。

Abstract: Grokking -- the delayed onset of generalization after early memorization -- is often described with phase-transition language, but that claim has lacked falsifiable finite-size inputs. Here we supply those inputs by treating the group order $p$ of $\mathbb{Z}_p$ as an admissible extensive variable and a held-out spectral head-tail contrast as a representation-level order parameter, then apply a condensed-matter-style diagnostic chain to coarse-grid sweeps and a dense near-critical addition audit. Binder-like crossings reveal a shared finite-size boundary, and susceptibility comparison strongly disfavors a smooth-crossover interpretation ($Δ\mathrm{AIC}=16.8$ in the near-critical audit). Phase-transition language in grokking can therefore be tested as a quantitative finite-size claim rather than invoked as analogy alone, although the transition order remains unresolved at present.

### [6] [How unconstrained machine-learning models learn physical symmetries](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.24638)
*Michelangelo Domina, Joseph William Abbott, Paolo Pegolo, Filippo Bigi, Michele Ceriotti*

Main category: cs.LG

TL;DR: 本文研究了未受约束的机器学习模型如何学习物理对称性，并提出了一种严格的框架来诊断模型中的谱失效模式，证明可以通过注入最小的归纳偏置来提高模型的稳定性和准确性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统机器学习模型为了保证物理预测的对称性，通常采用受约束的数学形式。然而，未受约束的模型在性能上具有竞争力，并且可以通过简单的数据增强策略学习近似的对称行为。因此，需要理解未受约束模型如何处理和学习对称信息。

Method: 研究人员引入了严格的指标来衡量学习表示中的对称内容，并评估输出满足对称条件的情况。他们将这些指标应用于基于Transformer的两个未受约束模型（原子模拟中的图神经网络和粒子物理中的PointNet架构），分析了对称信息如何在不同架构层中处理和学习。

Result: 研究发现，未受约束的模型能够学习近似的对称行为，并且可以通过数据增强策略实现高精度的预测。基于这些分析，研究人员建立了一个诊断谱失效模式的框架，并证明可以通过注入最小的归纳偏置来提高模型的稳定性和准确性。

Conclusion: 该研究表明，未受约束的机器学习模型可以有效地学习物理对称性，并且在保证物理保真度的同时，可以保留其高表达力和可扩展性。这为开发更高效、更具可扩展性的物理模拟模型提供了新的思路。

In this paper, we introduce rigorous metrics to measure the symmetry content of the learned representations in such models, and assess the accuracy by which the outputs fulfill the equivariant condition. We apply these metrics to two unconstrained, transformer-based models operating on decorated point clouds (a graph neural network for atomistic simulations and a PointNet-style architecture for particle physics) to investigate how symmetry information is processed across architectural layers and is learned during training. Based on these insights, we establish a rigorous framework for diagnosing spectral failure modes in ML models. Enabled by this analysis, we demonstrate that one can achieve superior stability and accuracy by strategically injecting the minimum required inductive biases, preserving the high expressivity and scalability of unconstrained architectures while guaranteeing physical fidelity.
