### [1] [ChemFlow:A Hierarchical Neural Network for Multiscale Representation Learning in Chemical Mixtures](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.02810)
*Jinming Fan, Chao Qian, Wilhelm T. S. Huck, William E. Robinson, Shaodong Zhou*

Main category: physics.chem-ph

TL;DR: ChemFlow是一种新的分层神经网络框架，用于预测复杂化学混合物的行为，它整合了原子、官能团和分子层面的特征，并能有效处理浓度依赖性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有图神经网络方法主要关注分子内的相互作用，难以模拟真实化学环境中的多尺度、稠密耦合的相互作用以及浓度对信息交换的调制。

Method: ChemFlow采用原子层面的特征融合模块Chem-embed生成上下文感知的原子表示，并利用双向官能团-分子和分子-官能团注意力机制捕捉混合物中官能团之间的相互作用。该模型动态调整表示，以适应浓度和组成的变化。

Result: ChemFlow在预测浓度依赖性性质方面表现出色，显著优于现有最先进的模型，并在浓度敏感型和非浓度敏感型系统中都表现出更高的准确性和效率。

Conclusion: ChemFlow为复杂化学混合物的建模提供了一种有效且高效的方法，有望加速材料发现和化学过程设计。

Abstract: Accurate prediction of the physicochemical properties of molecular mixtures using graph neural networks remains a significant challenge, as it requires simultaneous embedding of intramolecular interactions while accounting for mixture composition (i.e., concentrations and ratios). Existing approaches are ill-equipped to emulate realistic mixture environments, where densely coupled interactions propagate across hierarchical levels - from atoms and functional groups to entire molecules - and where cross-level information exchange is continuously modulated by composition. To bridge the gap between isolated molecules and realistic chemical environments, we present ChemFlow, a novel hierarchical framework that integrates atomic, functional group, and molecular-level features, facilitating information flow across these levels to predict the behavior of complex chemical mixtures. ChemFlow employs an atomic-level feature fusion module, Chem-embed, to generate context-aware atomic representations influenced by the mixture state and atomic characteristics. Next, bidirectional group-to-molecule and molecule-to-group attention mechanisms enable ChemFlow to capture functional group interactions both within and across molecules in the mixture. By dynamically adjusting representations based on concentration and composition, ChemFlow excels at predicting concentration-dependent properties and significantly outperforms state-of-the-art models in both concentration-sensitive and concentration-independent systems. Extensive experiments demonstrate ChemFlow's superior accuracy and efficiency in modeling complex chemical mixtures.

### [2] [On Geometry Regularization in Autoencoder Reduced-Order Models with Latent Neural ODE Dynamics](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03238)
*Mikhail Osipov*

Main category: cs.LG

TL;DR: 本文研究了自编码器中几何正则化策略对降阶模型的影响，发现某些正则化方法虽然改善了解码器的平滑性，反而使得学习潜在动态更加困难，而Stiefel投影则能改善潜在动态的条件诊断并提升性能。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 自编码器在降阶模型中广泛应用，但潜在空间维度降低会导致信息损失和解码器局部膨胀，从而放大潜在误差，尤其是在长时间演化中。因此，需要研究如何通过几何正则化来控制解码器的敏感性和几何性质。

Method: 在对对流-扩散-反应方程进行建模的实验设置中，使用神经网络ODE学习潜在动态。研究了四种自编码器预训练中的几何正则化方法：(a) 解码器雅可比矩阵的近等距正则化，(b) 基于随机方向增益的随机解码器增益惩罚，(c) 二阶方向曲率惩罚，(d) Stiefel投影应用于第一层解码器。

Result: 实验结果表明，(a)-(c)正则化方法通常会使后续的潜在动态学习更加困难，尤其是在长时间演化中，即使它们改善了局部解码器平滑性。而(d) Stiefel投影则始终能改善与条件相关的潜在动态诊断，并倾向于产生更好的演化性能。

Conclusion: 研究表明，在当前设置下，潜在几何不匹配的影响超过了改善解码器平滑性的好处。Stiefel投影是一种更有效的自编码器预训练方法，可以改善潜在动态的稳定性。

Abstract: We investigate geometric regularization strategies for learned latent representations in encoder--decoder reduced-order models. In a fixed experimental setting for the advection--diffusion--reaction (ADR) equation, we model latent dynamics using a neural ODE and evaluate four regularization approaches applied during autoencoder pre-training: (a) near-isometry regularization of the decoder Jacobian, (b) a stochastic decoder gain penalty based on random directional gains, (c) a second-order directional curvature penalty, and (d) Stiefel projection of the first decoder layer. Across multiple seeds, we find that (a)--(c) often produce latent representations that make subsequent latent-dynamics training with a frozen autoencoder more difficult, especially for long-horizon rollouts, even when they improve local decoder smoothness or related sensitivity proxies. In contrast, (d) consistently improves conditioning-related diagnostics of the learned latent dynamics and tends to yield better rollout performance. We discuss the hypothesis that, in this setting, the downstream impact of latent-geometry mismatch outweighs the benefits of improved decoder smoothness.

### [3] [Information Routing in Atomistic Foundation Models: How Equivariance Creates Linearly Disentangled Representations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03155)
*Joshua Steier*

Main category: cs.LG

TL;DR: 本研究引入了Composition Projection Decomposition (CPD)方法，分析了原子基础模型中信息组织的线性解缠结程度，发现张量积等变架构（MACE）产生的表示更易于线性访问几何信息，并揭示了模型内部信号的路由机制。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为了理解原子基础模型内部如何组织化学信息，以及这些模型是否将几何和成分信息以清晰可分离的方式编码，从而评估其在下游任务中的效率和透明度。

Method: 研究团队引入了基于QR分解的CPD方法，用于线性移除模型表示中的成分信号，并探查几何残差。他们对8个模型（来自5种架构家族）在QM9分子和Materials Project晶体数据集上进行了分析，并使用线性探针评估解缠结程度，同时还分析了不可约表示（irrep）特定的路由。

Result: 研究发现，张量积等变架构（MACE）产生的表示具有最高的线性解缠结程度（R2geom = 0.782），而手工设计的描述符（ANI-2x）则以非线性方式编码相同的信息。MACE将特定目标信号路由到特定的不可约表示通道（例如，偶极信号到L=1通道，HOMO-LUMO间隙信号到L=0通道），梯度提升树探针结果被系统性高估，线性探针是更可靠的评估指标。MACE的线性解缠结表示在线性探针下具有更高的样本效率。

Conclusion: 线性解缠结的表示能够提高样本效率，表明等变架构在原始预测准确率之外具有实际优势。该研究为理解和设计更高效、更透明的原子基础模型提供了新的视角，并强调了线性探针在评估模型内部表示解缠结程度的重要性。

Abstract: What do atomistic foundation models encode in their intermediate representations, and how is that information organized? We introduce Composition Projection Decomposition (CPD), which uses QR projection to linearly remove composition signal from learned representations and probes the geometric residual. Across eight models from five architectural families on QM9 molecules and Materials Project crystals, we find a disentanglement gradient: tensor product equivariant architectures (MACE) produce representations where geometry is almost fully linearly accessible after composition removal ($R^2_{\text{geom}} = 0.782$ for HOMO-LUMO gap), while handcrafted descriptors (ANI-2x) entangle the same information nonlinearly ($R^2_{\text{geom}} = -0.792$ under Ridge; $R^2 = +0.784$ under MLP). MACE routes target-specific signal through irreducible representation channels -- dipole to $L = 1$, HOMO-LUMO gap to $L = 0$ -- a pattern not observed in ViSNet's vector-scalar architecture under the same probe. We show that gradient boosted tree probes on projected residuals are systematically inflated, recovering $R^2 = 0.68$--$0.95$ on a purely compositional target, and recommend linear probes as the primary metric. Linearly disentangled representations are more sample-efficient under linear probing, suggesting a practical advantage for equivariant architectures beyond raw prediction accuracy.

</details>
