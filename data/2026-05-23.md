### [1] [Thermodynamic Irreversibility of Training Algorithms](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21933)
*Liu Ziyin, Yuanjie Ren, Adam Levine, Isaac Chuang*

Main category: cond-mat.stat-mech

TL;DR: 本文建立了一个分析神经网络训练算法不可逆性的通用框架，证明了四种不同的不可逆性量化方法等价，并揭示了不可逆性对训练过程的影响。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 理解现代AI系统的学习动态是人工智能研究的重要任务。神经网络训练过程可以视为非平衡过程，分析其不可逆性有助于应用物理学工具研究训练动态。

Method: 研究人员考虑了通用离散时间动态，并定义了四种不同的不可逆性量化方法：数值后向误差、时间重整化修正、微观时间反转不对称性和随机热力学熵产生。他们分析了这些方法在小步长下的等价性。

Result: 研究表明，四种不可逆性量化方法在小步长下等价，不可逆性导致时间反转对称性破缺，产生一种新兴力，该力会破坏非等距连续重参数化对称性，保留正交对称性，并导致对最小化熵产生率的学习轨迹的普遍偏好。

Conclusion: 本文建立了一个分析神经网络训练算法不可逆性的理论框架，为理解和优化深度学习算法提供了新的视角，并为将物理学工具应用于人工智能研究开辟了道路。

Abstract: The training algorithms for AI systems all introduce far-from-equilibrium dynamical processes, and understanding the irreversibility of these algorithms is a fundamental step towards understanding the learning dynamics of modern AI systems. In this work, we establish a general framework for defining and analyzing the irreversibility of training algorithms. We show that four different ways to characterize the irreversibility of dynamical processes are equivalent to leading order in the step size $η$: numerical backward error $φ_{\rm DE}$, time-renormalized correction $φ_{\rm TR}$, microscopic time reversal asymmetry $φ_{\rm TA}$, and the (regularized) stochastic-thermodynamic entropy production $φ_{\rm ST}$. The irreversibility gives rise to a time-reversal-symmetry-breaking emergent force that generically breaks non-isometric continuous reparametrization symmetries, preserves orthogonal symmetries, and leads to a universal preference for those learning trajectories that minimize the entropy production rate.

### [2] [MetaDNS: Enhancing Exploration in Discrete Neural Samplers via Well-Tempered Metadynamics](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21722)
*Xiaochen Du, Juno Nam, Jaemoo Choi, Wei Guo, Sathya Edamadaka, Junyi Sha, Elton Pan, Yongxin Chen, Molei Tao, Rafael Gómez-Bombarelli*

Main category: cond-mat.stat-mech

TL;DR: MetaDNS是一种将well-tempered metadynamics融入离散神经网络采样器的新框架，旨在解决现有采样器在探索高能量区域和多模态分布时的困难。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有的离散神经网络采样器（如MDNS）容易出现模式坍塌，无法有效采样高能量区域，导致自由能估计和相变理解方面的不足，尤其是在复杂材料系统中评估能量函数计算成本高昂的情况下。

Method: MetaDNS通过在选定的低维坐标上维护自适应、依赖历史的偏差势函数，将well-tempered metadynamics整合到离散扩散或自回归采样器中。这种偏差势引导采样器探索先前无法访问的区域。

Result: 在Ising、Potts和铜-金二元合金等具有挑战性的低温基准测试中，MetaDNS成功重现了热力学分布，并且在探索效率上与基于MCMC的metadynamics方法相当，所需的偏差沉积步骤更少。

Conclusion: MetaDNS提供了一个通用的框架，可以显著增强离散神经网络采样器的探索能力，使其能够更准确地估计自由能，理解相变，并为材料科学等领域提供更可靠的预测。

Abstract: Sampling from discrete distributions with multiple modes and energy barriers is fundamental to machine learning and computational physics. Recent discrete neural samplers like MDNS suffer from mode collapse and fail to sample high-energy barrier regions between modes, which is critical for free energy estimation and understanding phase transitions. We propose Metadynamics Discrete Neural Sampler (MetaDNS), a general framework integrating well-tempered metadynamics into discrete diffusion or autoregressive samplers. By maintaining an adaptive, history-dependent bias potential along selected low-dimensional coordinates, MetaDNS forces exploration of previously inaccessible regions, enabling free energy reconstruction infeasible with standard neural samplers due to a lack of high-energy samples. On challenging low-temperature benchmarks including Ising, Potts, and the copper-gold binary alloy, MetaDNS reproduces the thermodynamic distribution. Compared to MCMC-based metadynamics, MetaDNS also achieves comparable exploration requiring fewer bias deposition steps.

### [3] [Atom-level Protein Representation Learning Improves Protein Structure Prediction](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.22133)
*Taewon Kim, Hyosoon Jang, Hyunjin Seo, Seonghwan Seo, Hyeongwoo Kim, Wonho Zhung, Mingyeong Shin, Wooyoun Kim, Sungsoo Ahn*

Main category: q-bio.BM

TL;DR: 本文提出了TRIPROREP，一种结构感知的预训练蛋白质表示模型，通过联合建模氨基酸序列、骨架几何和全原子几何信息，显著提升了蛋白质结构预测性能。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有蛋白质表示学习方法在评估结构感知能力方面存在不足，难以有效衡量表示是否包含预测蛋白质结构所需的三维几何信息。本文旨在研究预训练蛋白质表示是否能作为有用的几何信号，用于结构预测建模。

Method: TRIPROREP采用VQ-VAE分词器将蛋白质的氨基酸序列、骨架几何和全原子几何信息分别离散化为token序列，并使用ELECTRA风格的判别式预训练方法，通过生成器对token序列进行扰动，然后训练判别器预测原始token，从而学习区分合理的但错误的跨视图增强与原始蛋白质。

Result: 在REPSP基准测试中，TRIPROREP在同二聚体的共折叠、残基级别的相互作用属性预测以及表示对齐的单体结构预测等任务中，优于仅使用序列信息的模型和先前的结构感知表示模型，同时保持在传统基准上的竞争力。

Conclusion: 本文的研究表明，预训练的蛋白质表示可以作为有用的几何信号，用于结构预测建模，并为蛋白质表示学习的评估和应用提供了新的视角和方法。

Abstract: Recent advances in generative modeling show that pretrained representations can improve generation as conditioning features or alignment targets. Motivated by this, we study protein representations for predicting structures beyond conventional function annotation. We propose TriProRep, a structure-aware pretraining method that jointly models three aligned residue-level views: amino-acid identity, backbone geometry, and local full-atom geometry, discretely encoded via VQ-VAE tokenizers. By pretraining to recover original tokens from generator-corrupted views, TriProRep learns to distinguish plausible but incorrect cross-view augmentations from the original protein. We further introduce RepSP, a benchmark for evaluating protein representations in structure-predictive settings. RepSP tests three uses of representations: homodimer co-folding from apo-chain representations, residue-level prediction of homodimer-derived interaction properties, and representation-aligned monomer structure prediction. Across these tasks, TriProRep improves over sequence-only and prior structure-aware representation models, while maintaining competitive performance on conventional benchmarks.

### [4] [Posterior Collapse as Automatic Spectral Pruning](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.22691)
*Johannes Hirn*

Main category: cs.LG

TL;DR: 本文研究了β-VAE中的后验坍缩现象，并证明了它实际上是自动谱剪枝的一种形式，揭示了潜在模式的逐级解耦过程。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统的VAE文献通常将后验坍缩视为一种失败模式，本文旨在重新审视这一现象，将其视为一种可测量的响应，并探索其在特征学习和降维等方面的潜在价值。

Method: 通过对损失函数的Landau稳定性分析，作者定义了一个与潜变量重缩放无关的序参量，用于对活跃的潜变量进行排序，并确定需要首先检查的有效变量。在线性高斯情况下，作者分析了坍缩谱、效用谱和归一化PCA谱之间的关系。

Result: 研究发现，在线性高斯情况下，坍缩谱、效用谱和归一化PCA谱一致，并且每次坍缩都遵循均场定律。通过在World-Clim数据集上的实验验证了这些预测。

Conclusion: 本文将VAE与信息瓶颈程序联系起来，揭示了VAE中隐藏的模式级贸易和不稳定性，为理解和利用后验坍缩提供了新的视角，并为特征学习和降维提供了潜在的指导。

Abstract: We show that posterior collapse in $β$-VAEs implements automatic spectral pruning. A latent mode collapses if its contribution to reconstruction is below the cutoff set by $β$. Equilibrium solutions with different $β$ thus reveal a cascade of collapses as latent modes decouple from least to most useful.   We derive this as a consequence of the loss via a Landau stability analysis. We define a latent-rescaling-invariant order parameter that ranks active latent modes and whose collapse thresholds identify which effective variables to inspect first.   In the linear Gaussian case, the collapse spectrum, utility spectrum, and normalized PCA spectrum coincide, and each collapse follows a mean-field law. We test these predictions on the WorldClim dataset.

</details>
