### [1] [Path Sampling for Rare Events Boosted by Machine Learning](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.05167)
*Porhouy Minh, Sapna Sarupria*

Main category: physics.comp-ph

TL;DR: 本文评论了Jung等人提出的AIMMD方法，该方法利用机器学习增强过渡路径采样(TPS)的效率，实现分子自组装机制的发现。AIMMD能够实时估计提交概率并推导可供人类理解的反应坐标。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 揭示复杂反应机制是化学和生物科学中的一个核心挑战。传统的分子动力学模拟难以直接采样稀有事件，因此需要增强采样技术。

Method: AIMMD将机器学习与TPS框架相结合，通过机器学习实时学习反应坐标（提交概率），并同时推导可供人类理解的反应坐标，从而避免了传统TPS方法依赖于预先选择的集体变量的局限性。

Result: AIMMD提供了一个强大的框架，用于阐明复杂分子过程的机制路径，并具有在实践中提高采样效率的潜力。

Conclusion: AIMMD方法为分子机制发现提供了一种有前景的途径，有望克服传统增强采样技术面临的挑战，但同时也需要进一步研究其潜在的局限性。

Abstract: The study by Jung et al. (Jung H, Covino R, Arjun A, et al., Nat Comput Sci. 3:334-345 (2023)) introduced Artificial Intelligence for Molecular Mechanism Discovery (AIMMD), a novel sampling algorithm that integrates machine learning to enhance the efficiency of transition path sampling (TPS). By enabling on-the-fly estimation of the committor probability and simultaneously deriving a human-interpretable reaction coordinate, AIMMD offers a robust framework for elucidating the mechanistic pathways of complex molecular processes. This commentary provides a discussion and critical analysis of the core AIMMD framework, explores its recent extensions, and offers an assessment of the method's potential impact and limitations.

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种名为“尺度感知可解释性”的统一研究议程，旨在借鉴物理学中的重整化框架，为神经网络的可解释性提供更稳健和忠实的保证，尤其关注尺度信息。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有的神经网络可解释性方法缺乏对尺度信息（分辨率）的有效处理，难以保证在忽略细粒度结构时，对模型行为的影响边界，因此需要一种能够显式跟踪特征在不同分辨率下如何组合的方法。

Method: 借鉴物理学中的重整化框架，提出一种尺度感知可解释性框架，该框架旨在：1) 在神经网络中寻找自然尺度进行特征粗加工；2) 提取尺度相关的特征相关性；3) 提供诊断或保证，以确定在特定抽象级别下可以忽略细粒度波动。

Result: 本文概述了重整化框架在神经网络中的应用，并提出了一个统一的研究议程，呼吁物理学、神经科学、计算机科学和人工智能安全社区之间的协调合作，以发展具有统计物理支持的正式机制和可解释性工具。

Abstract: Neural networks organize information according to the hierarchical, multi-scale structure of natural data. Methods to interpret model internals should be similarly scale-aware, explicitly tracking how features compose across resolutions and guaranteeing bounds on the influence of fine-grained structure that is discarded as irrelevant noise. We posit that the renormalisation framework from physics can meet this need by offering technical tools that can overcome limitations of current methods. Moreover, relevant work from adjacent fields has now matured to a point where scattered research threads can be synthesized into practical, theory-informed tools. To combine these threads in an AI safety context, we propose a unifying research agenda -- \emph{scale-aware interpretability} -- to develop formal machinery and interpretability tools that have robustness and faithfulness properties supported by statistical physics.

### [3] [Stochastic hierarchical data-driven optimization: application to plasma-surface kinetics](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.04975)
*José Afonso, Vasco Guerra, Pedro Viegas*

Main category: cs.LG

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种基于Sloppy Model理论的随机分层优化框架，用于高效校准物理模型，特别适用于计算成本高的复杂反应系统。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统优化方法在处理高维参数空间和计算成本高的模拟器时效率低下，难以从有限数据中准确确定物理参数。研究旨在解决在计算资源有限的情况下，如何高效地优化复杂模型的参数。

Method: 该框架利用减少的Hessian近似来识别和优化关键参数子空间，避免了穷举采样。结合概率公式，直接从观测数据推导目标损失函数，并采用随机分层优化算法，将优化问题视为几何推理任务。

Result: 在等离子体-表面相互作用问题上的验证表明，该方法在样本效率方面优于基线优化技术，能够更有效地利用有限的模拟数据。

Conclusion: 该方法提供了一种通用且可扩展的工具，可用于优化从等离子体化学到生物化学网络的复杂反应系统的模型，具有广泛的应用前景。

Abstract: This work introduces a stochastic hierarchical optimization framework inspired by Sloppy Model theory for the efficient calibration of physical models. Central to this method is the use of a reduced Hessian approximation, which identifies and targets the stiff parameter subspace using minimal simulation queries. This strategy enables efficient navigation of highly anisotropic landscapes, avoiding the computational burden of exhaustive sampling. To ensure rigorous inference, we integrate this approach with a probabilistic formulation that derives a principled objective loss function directly from observed data. We validate the framework by applying it to the problem of plasma-surface interactions, where accurate modelling is strictly limited by uncertainties in surface reactivity parameters and the computational cost of kinetic simulations. Comparative analysis demonstrates that our method consistently outperforms baseline optimization techniques in sample efficiency. This approach offers a general and scalable tool for optimizing models of complex reaction systems, ranging from plasma chemistry to biochemical networks.

### [4] [Mechanisms of AI Protein Folding in ESMFold](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.06020)
*Kevin Lu, Jannik Brinkmann, Stefan Huber, Aaron Mueller, Yonatan Belinkov, David Bau, Chris Wendler*

Main category: cs.LG

TL;DR: 本文研究了ESMFold蛋白质折叠的机制，通过干预模型潜在表示，揭示了其折叠过程中的两个计算阶段，并阐明了这些阶段如何处理生物化学特征和空间特征。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 理解AI蛋白质折叠模型（如ESMFold）如何从氨基酸序列计算出三维结构，以及在推断过程中结构决策何时做出，对于理解、诊断和改进此类模型至关重要。

Method: 研究人员使用激活迁移（activation patching）技术，将包含β-发夹的蛋白质的序列和成对表示从前向传播移植到包含α-螺旋区域的目标蛋白质的前向传播中。通过观察迁移后的折叠树干是否产生发夹，并改变迁移的内容（序列与成对表示）和位置（早期与晚期块），来定位和表征驱动β-发夹形成的计算。

Result: 研究发现ESMFold的折叠过程分为两个计算阶段：早期阶段初始化成对生物化学信号（如氨基酸身份和电荷），将序列表示传递到成对表示；晚期阶段发展成对空间特征（如距离和接触信息），利用积累的生物化学信息。

Conclusion: 该研究首次对蛋白质模型折叠树干进行了机制分析，揭示了ESMFold的结构决策可以被定位、追踪和操纵，为理解和改进AI蛋白质折叠模型提供了重要见解。

Abstract: How do protein structure prediction models fold proteins? We investigate this question by tracing how ESMFold folds a beta hairpin, a prevalent structural motif. Through counterfactual interventions on model latents, we identify two computational stages in the folding trunk. In the first stage, early blocks initialize pairwise biochemical signals: residue identities and associated biochemical features such as charge flow from sequence representations into pairwise representations. In the second stage, late blocks develop pairwise spatial features: distance and contact information accumulate in the pairwise representation. We demonstrate that the mechanisms underlying structural decisions of ESMFold can be localized, traced through interpretable representations, and manipulated with strong causal effects.

### [5] [Graph--Theoretic Analysis of Phase Optimization Complexity in Variational Wave Functions for Heisenberg Antiferromagnets](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.04943)
*Mahmud Ashraf Shamim, Moshiur Rahman, Mohamed Hibat-Allah, Paulo T Araujo*

Main category: cond-mat.str-el

TL;DR: 本文将受挫海森堡反铁磁体的波函数相位重建问题建模为图论中的加权最大割问题，并证明该问题是NP困难的，揭示了波函数符号结构与组合优化之间的联系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 受挫海森堡反铁磁体（HAF）的波函数相位结构难以系统表征，限制了对该类系统的理解。本文旨在研究相位重建问题的复杂性，并提供一种结构化的视角。

Method: 将HAF的希尔伯特空间表示为加权图（希尔伯特图HG），顶点代表自旋构型，边由海森堡哈密顿量的非对角线自旋翻转项生成，权重由波函数振幅的乘积决定。在固定振幅并限制相位为Z2值的情况下，相位相关的变分能量可以被重新表述为HG上的加权Ising反铁磁体，相位重建问题转化为加权最大割问题。

Result: 证明了HAF的相位重建问题是NP困难的，并且揭示了波函数符号结构与组合优化之间的直接联系。通过图论分析，得出了关于全局相位/符号一致性的结构标准。

Conclusion: 本文提供了一种新的图论视角来理解受挫HAF的相位重建问题，为设计更有效的变分波函数方法和理解复杂量子系统提供了理论基础。

Abstract: Despite extensive study, the phase structure of the wavefunctions in frustrated Heisenberg antiferromagnets (HAF) is not yet systematically characterized. In this work, we represent the Hilbert space of an HAF as a weighted graph, which we term the Hilbert graph (HG), whose vertices are spin configurations and whose edges are generated by off-diagonal spin-flip terms of the Heisenberg Hamiltonian, with weights set by products of wavefunction amplitudes. Holding the amplitudes fixed and restricting phases to $\mathbb{Z}_2$ values, the phase-dependent variational energy can be recast as a classical Ising antiferromagnet on the HG, so that phase reconstruction of the ground state reduces to a weighted Max-Cut instance. This shows that phase reconstruction HAF is worst-case NP-hard and provides a direct link between wavefunction sign structure and combinatorial optimization.

</details>
