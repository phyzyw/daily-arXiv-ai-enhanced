### [2] [Physics-Informed Neural Networks for Solving Derivative-Constrained PDEs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.13723)
*Kentaro Hoshisashi, Carolyn E Phelan, Paolo Barucca*

Main category: cs.LG

TL;DR: 本文提出了Derivative-Constrained PINNs (DC-PINNs)，一种新的框架，用于解决具有导数约束的偏微分方程(PDEs)。DC-PINNs将约束条件融入优化目标，通过最小化目标函数来寻找物理上合理的解。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统的PINNs在处理包含额外导数约束的PDEs时面临挑战，这些约束在实际应用中至关重要，例如边界、单调性、凸性和不可压缩性等。现有方法容易导致训练不稳定、收敛缓慢以及约束违反。

Method: DC-PINNs将导数约束显式地编码到优化目标中，并采用自适应损失平衡技术来调整每个目标的权重，减少对手动超参数调整的依赖。该框架利用自动微分高效计算导数约束，并基于能量最小原理进行优化。

Result: 实验结果表明，DC-PINNs在解决具有约束条件的PDEs时，能够显著减少约束违反，提高物理保真度，并在热扩散、金融波动率和流体流动等基准测试中表现优于传统的PINN方法。

Conclusion: DC-PINNs提供了一种稳定且可靠的求解受约束PDEs的方法，通过显式编码导数约束，优化过程能够引导至物理上可行的极小值，为PINNs在更广泛的实际应用中提供了新的可能性。

Abstract: Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs) recast PDE solving as an optimisation problem in function space by minimising a residual-based objective, yet many applications require additional derivative-based relations that are just as fundamental as the governing equations. In this paper, we present Derivative-Constrained PINNs (DC-PINNs), a general framework that treats constrained PDE solving as an optimisation guided by a minimum objective function criterion where the physics resides in the minimum principle. DC-PINNs embed general nonlinear constraints on states and derivatives, e.g., bounds, monotonicity, convexity, incompressibility, computed efficiently via automatic differentiation, and they employ self-adaptive loss balancing to tune the influence of each objective, reducing reliance on manual hyperparameters and problem-specific architectures. DC-PINNs consistently reduce constraint violations and improve physical fidelity versus baseline PINN variants, representative hard-constraint formulations on benchmarks, including heat diffusion with bounds, financial volatilities with arbitrage-free, and fluid flow with vortices shed. Explicitly encoding derivative constraints stabilises training and steers optimisation toward physically admissible minima even when the PDE residual alone is small, providing reliable solutions of constrained PDEs grounded in energy minimum principles.

### [3] [Rare Event Analysis via Stochastic Optimal Control](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.13213)
*Yuanqi Du, Jiajun He, Dinghuai Zhang, Eric Vanden-Eijnden, Carles Domingo-Enrich*

Main category: stat.ML

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种基于随机最优控制的罕见事件分析框架，将计算突变路径理论中的提交函数问题转化为一个控制问题，从而更有效地采样罕见路径。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 罕见事件（如生物分子构象变化、相变和化学反应）在物理系统中至关重要，但由于其发生的频率极低，直接数值模拟难以捕捉，因此需要更有效的分析方法。

Method: 将提交函数估计问题转化为一个随机最优控制问题，通过反馈控制（与提交函数梯度成正比）主动引导轨迹到达反应区域。开发了反向传播损失和价值匹配损失两种互补目标函数，并解决了中间盆地陷阱问题，通过降低有效势垒来保留反应电流。

Result: 该框架在基准系统上，提交函数估计、反应速率和平衡常数的准确性显著优于现有方法。

Conclusion: 该研究提供了一种新的罕见事件分析方法，通过将突变路径理论与随机最优控制相结合，能够更准确地估计罕见事件的动力学和热力学性质，为相关领域的研究提供新的工具。

Abstract: Rare events such as conformational changes in biomolecules, phase transitions, and chemical reactions are central to the behavior of many physical systems, yet they are extremely difficult to study computationally because unbiased simulations seldom produce them. Transition Path Theory (TPT) provides a rigorous statistical framework for analyzing such events: it characterizes the ensemble of reactive trajectories between two designated metastable states (reactant and product), and its central object--the committor function, which gives the probability that the system will next reach the product rather than the reactant--encodes all essential kinetic and thermodynamic information. We introduce a framework that casts committor estimation as a stochastic optimal control (SOC) problem. In this formulation the committor defines a feedback control--proportional to the gradient of its logarithm--that actively steers trajectories toward the reactive region, thereby enabling efficient sampling of reactive paths. To solve the resulting hitting-time control problem we develop two complementary objectives: a direct backpropagation loss and a principled off-policy Value Matching loss, for which we establish first-order optimality guarantees. We further address metastability, which can trap controlled trajectories in intermediate basins, by introducing an alternative sampling process that preserves the reactive current while lowering effective energy barriers. On benchmark systems, the framework yields markedly more accurate committor estimates, reaction rates, and equilibrium constants than existing methods.

### [4] [Training single-electron and single-photon stochastic physical neural networks](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.10861)

Main category: quant-ph

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种基于单电子隧穿和单光子源的新型随机物理神经网络（PNN）模型，并通过实验验证了其在MNIST手写数字识别任务中具有高准确率，即使在存在大量噪声和模型不确定性的情况下也能保持良好性能。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 深度学习的计算成本日益增加，促使人们探索替代计算方法。物理神经网络（PNN）通过利用物理过程直接执行学习和推理，有望实现更节能和更快速的计算。然而，PNN在存在设备非理想情况下的训练是一个挑战。

Method: 研究人员设计了基于单电子隧穿和单光子源的随机神经元模型，并使用这些模型构建单隐藏层随机PNN，用于MNIST手写数字分类。他们探索了不同的训练策略，包括调整每层试验次数以控制前向传播的随机性，以及在反向传播中使用真实概率或经验输出来评估其对梯度估计的影响。

Result: 实验结果表明，当在反向传播中使用经验输出时，网络能够以超过97%的测试准确率完成MNIST手写数字识别任务，即使每层试验次数较少，并且存在高噪声和模型不确定性。

Abstract: The computational demands of deep learning motivate the investigation of alternative approaches to computation. One alternative is physical neural networks~(PNNs), in which learning and inference are performed directly via physical processes. Stochastic PNNs arise when the underlying neurons are realized by the dynamics of a stochastic activation switch. Here we propose novel electronic and photonic stochastic neurons. The photonic realization is implemented via a single-photon source driving one of two modes coupled via a controllable beam-splitter-like interaction. In the electronic case, the charge state of the quantum dot forms the basis for the stochastic neuron, whereas in the photonic case the occupation of the undriven mode serves as the basis for the stochastic neuron. Training of stochastic PNNs is performed with models of stochastic neurons, as well as with coherently-driven, single-photon detector stochastic neurons previously introduced. Several training strategies for MNIST handwritten digit classification have been investigated using single-hidden-layer stochastic PNNs, including varying the number of trials in each layer to control forward pass stochasticity and employing either true probability or empirical outputs in the backward pass to evaluate their influence on gradient estimation. We show that when empirical outputs are used in the backward pass, the network achieves more than 97\% test accuracy with few trials per layer. Despite the simplicity of the model architecture, high test accuracy is maintained in the presence of a high degree of noise and model uncertainty. The results demonstrate the potential of embracing stochastic PNNs for deep learning.

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统的流固耦合模拟计算成本高昂，难以应用于参数空间探索、设计优化和实时控制等场景。因此，需要开发高效的数据驱动代理模型来替代高保真模拟。

Method: AeTHERON采用异构图神经网络架构，直接反映了浸没边界方法（IBM）的结构，使用稀疏交叉注意力机制耦合流体和结构域。通过连续正弦时间嵌入实现时间泛化，并在参数网格上训练模型。

Result: 在拍打柔性尾鳍的流固耦合模拟中，AeTHERON能够捕捉到大尺度涡流拓扑和尾流结构，在未见数据上的外推平均绝对误差（MAE）为0.168，在流动重组最快的半拍周期过渡处误差略有增加。

This physics-informed inductive bias enables AeTHERON to learn nonlinear fluid-structure coupling in a shared high-dimensional latent space, with continuous sinusoidal time embeddings providing temporal generalization across lead times. Inference requires milliseconds per timestep on a single GPU versus hours for equivalent DNS computation. This is a continuously developing preprint; results and figures will be updated in subsequent versions.

### [6] [Nested Fourier-enhanced neural operator for efficient modeling of radiation transfer in fires](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.13919)

Main category: physics.flu-dyn

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种名为Nested Fourier-MIONet的机器学习框架，用于高效地模拟火灾中的辐射传递，该框架结合了傅里叶变换和神经网络算子，在保证精度的同时显著提高了计算效率。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统的辐射传递方程求解方法计算成本高昂，成为火灾模拟中的性能瓶颈。为了解决这个问题，需要一种既高效又准确的替代方案。

Method: 研究人员提出了基于傅里叶增强的多输入神经网络算子（Fourier-MIONet）的机器学习框架。为了处理多层网格精化的3D火灾模拟，他们进一步提出了嵌套的Fourier-MIONet，用于在不同网格层级之间训练和推断辐射解。

With fast and accurate inference, the surrogate makes higher-fidelity radiation treatments practical and enables the incorporation of more spectrally resolved radiation models into CFD fire simulations for engineering applications.
