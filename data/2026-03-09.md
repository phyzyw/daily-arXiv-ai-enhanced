### [1] [On the Value of Tokeniser Pretraining in Physics Foundation Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.05598)
*Hadi Sotoudeh, Payel Mukhopadhyay, Ruben Ohana, Michael McCabe, Neil D. Lawrence, Shirley Ho, Miles Cranmer*

Main category: cs.LG

TL;DR: 本文研究了在物理基础模型中预训练tokeniser对提高计算效率和准确性的影响。实验表明，在相同物理系统上预训练tokeniser可以显著提升性能，并提供了选择预训练数据的实用指导。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现代高分辨率模拟产生大量数据，训练物理基础模型需要高效的方法。现有的模型通常同时学习数据表示和物理动态，但本文认为预训练tokeniser可以提高效率，并探索了预训练数据选择的重要性。

Method: 研究人员系统性地评估了tokeniser预训练对物理基础模型的影响，采用了自编码目标进行预训练，并引入了灵活的时空压缩操作。对比了在相同物理系统和不同物理系统上预训练的效果，以及与从头开始训练的对比。

Result: 在相同物理系统上预训练tokeniser，相比从头开始训练，VRMSE降低了64%。实验结果表明，领域对齐的预训练效果最佳，不同系统预训练也能带来一定提升。

Conclusion: 本文首次系统性地研究了物理基础模型中tokeniser预训练的价值，为训练高效的物理模拟器提供了实践指导，并强调了战略性预训练数据选择的重要性。

Abstract: We investigate the impact of tokeniser pretraining on the accuracy and efficiency of physics emulation. Modern high-resolution simulations produce vast volumes of data spanning diverse physical regimes and scales. Training foundation models to learn the dynamics underlying such data enables the modelling of complex multiphysics phenomena, especially in data-limited settings. The emerging class of physics foundation models typically aims to learn two tasks jointly: (i) extracting compact representations of high-resolution spatiotemporal data, and (ii) capturing governing physical dynamics. However, learning both tasks from scratch simultaneously can impede the effectiveness of either process. We demonstrate that pretraining the tokeniser with an autoencoding objective prior to training the dynamics model enhances computational efficiency for downstream tasks. Notably, the magnitude of this benefit depends on domain alignment: pretraining on the same physical system as the downstream task yields the largest improvements, while pretraining on other systems provides moderate gains. In-domain pretraining reduces VRMSE by 64% after 10,500 training steps compared to training from scratch. To our knowledge, this is the first systematic investigation of tokeniser pretraining for physics foundation models. We further introduce flexible spatiotemporal compression operations that extend causal convolutions to support runtime-adjustable compression ratios, enabling efficient adaptation to diverse downstream tasks. Our findings provide practical guidance for training efficient physics emulators and highlight the importance of strategic pretraining data selection.

### [2] [Towards Efficient and Stable Ocean State Forecasting: A Continuous-Time Koopman Approach](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.05560)
*Rares Grozavescu, Pengyu Zhang, Mark Girolami, Etienne Meunier*

Main category: cs.LG

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种基于连续时间Koopman自编码器（CT-KAE）的海洋状态预测方法，该方法通过将非线性动力学投影到线性ODE控制的潜在空间，实现了高效且稳定的长期预测。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有的基于大型自回归架构的数据驱动海洋状态预测模型在长期预测中容易出现不稳定现象，而高保真度的全球气候模型计算成本过高。因此，需要一种计算效率高且稳定的替代方案。

Method: 研究人员将CT-KAE应用于一个双层准地转流（QG）模型，通过在潜在空间中利用线性常微分方程（ODE）进行演化，强制执行结构化的时序演化，并利用矩阵指数公式实现时间分辨率不变的预测。

Result: CT-KAE在2083天的时间尺度上表现出误差增长的界限和稳定的 крупномасштабные 统计特性，优于自回归Transformer基线模型。虽然精细尺度湍流结构部分耗散，但整体能量谱、enstrophy演化和自相关结构在较长的时间范围内保持一致，并且推理速度比数值求解器快几个数量级。

Conclusion: 连续时间Koopman代理模型为构建高效且稳定的混合物理-机器学习气候模型提供了一种有前景的骨干架构，有望加速海洋状态预测和气候研究。

Abstract: We investigate the Continuous-Time Koopman Autoencoder (CT-KAE) as a lightweight surrogate model for long-horizon ocean state forecasting in a two-layer quasi-geostrophic (QG) system. By projecting nonlinear dynamics into a latent space governed by a linear ordinary differential equation, the model enforces structured and interpretable temporal evolution while enabling temporally resolution-invariant forecasting via a matrix exponential formulation. Across 2083-day rollouts, CT-KAE exhibits bounded error growth and stable large-scale statistics, in contrast to autoregressive Transformer baselines which exhibit gradual error amplification and energy drift over long rollouts. While fine-scale turbulent structures are partially dissipated, bulk energy spectra, enstrophy evolution, and autocorrelation structure remain consistent over long horizons. The model achieves orders-of-magnitude faster inference compared to the numerical solver, suggesting that continuous-time Koopman surrogates offer a promising backbone for efficient and stable hybrid physical-machine learning climate models.

### [3] [Latent Diffusion-Based 3D Molecular Recovery from Vibrational Spectra](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.06113)
*Wenjin Wu, Aleš Leonardis, Linjiang Chen, Jianbo Jiao*

Main category: cs.LG

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种名为IR-GeoDiff的潜在扩散模型，能够从红外光谱中恢复三维分子几何结构，并能关注红外光谱中特征官能团区域。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有方法通常使用一维SMILES字符串或二维分子图来恢复分子结构，无法捕捉光谱特征与三维分子几何之间的复杂关系。扩散模型在生成三维分子结构方面取得了进展，但尚未探索对应于单个红外光谱的三维分子几何分布。

Method: IR-GeoDiff模型将光谱信息整合到分子结构的节点和边表示中，利用潜在扩散模型从红外光谱中恢复三维分子几何结构。模型通过注意力机制分析，能够聚焦于红外光谱中特征官能团区域。

Result: 实验结果表明，IR-GeoDiff模型能够从光谱和结构两个方面恢复给定红外光谱对应的分子分布，并且注意力机制分析结果与常见的化学解释实践一致。

Conclusion: IR-GeoDiff模型为从红外光谱中直接恢复三维分子结构提供了一种新方法，有望加速材料设计等领域的分子筛选和验证工作流程。

Abstract: Infrared (IR) spectroscopy, a type of vibrational spectroscopy, is widely used for molecular structure determination and provides critical structural information for chemists. However, existing approaches for recovering molecular structures from IR spectra typically rely on one-dimensional SMILES strings or two-dimensional molecular graphs, which fail to capture the intricate relationship between spectral features and three-dimensional molecular geometry. Recent advances in diffusion models have greatly enhanced the ability to generate molecular structures in 3D space. Yet, no existing model has explored the distribution of 3D molecular geometries corresponding to a single IR spectrum. In this work, we introduce IR-GeoDiff, a latent diffusion model that recovers 3D molecular geometries from IR spectra by integrating spectral information into both node and edge representations of molecular structures. We evaluate IR-GeoDiff from both spectral and structural perspectives, demonstrating its ability to recover the molecular distribution corresponding to a given IR spectrum. Furthermore, an attention-based analysis reveals that the model is able to focus on characteristic functional group regions in IR spectra, qualitatively consistent with common chemical interpretation practices.

### [4] [A recipe for scalable attention-based MLIPs: unlocking long-range accuracy with all-to-all node attention](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.06567)
*Eric Qu, Brandon M. Wood, Aditi S. Krishnapriyan, Zachary W. Ulissi*

Main category: cs.LG

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种名为AllScAIP的基于注意力机制的机器学习原子势模型，该模型能够有效捕捉长程相互作用，并在大规模数据集上实现了先进的能量/力准确率，并支持稳定的长时程分子动力学模拟。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有机器学习原子势模型在处理大型系统（如生物分子和电解质）时，难以准确捕捉长程相互作用，通常需要依赖显式的物理项或组件。本文旨在解决这一问题，开发一种能够有效扩展并捕捉长程相互作用的模型。

Method: AllScAIP模型采用一种简单直接的基于注意力的架构，包含一个全连接节点注意力组件，用于捕捉长程相互作用。该模型在数据驱动下学习长程相互作用，并根据数据量和模型大小调整物理偏置。

Result: AllScAIP模型在分子系统上实现了先进的能量/力准确率，在材料和催化剂评估中表现出竞争力。此外，该模型能够进行稳定的长时程分子动力学模拟，并准确预测实验观测值，如密度和汽化热。

Conclusion: AllScAIP模型证明了在模型规模增大时，长程相互作用可以通过数据驱动的注意力机制进行学习，而无需过度依赖显式的物理偏置。该模型为大规模机器学习原子势的开发提供了一种新的思路，并为模拟复杂分子系统提供了强大的工具。

Abstract: Machine-learning interatomic potentials (MLIPs) have advanced rapidly, with many top models relying on strong physics-based inductive biases. However, as models scale to larger systems like biomolecules and electrolytes, they struggle to accurately capture long-range (LR) interactions, leading current approaches to rely on explicit physics-based terms or components. In this work, we propose AllScAIP, a straightforward, attention-based, and energy-conserving MLIP model that scales to O(100 million) training samples. It addresses the long-range challenge using an all-to-all node attention component that is data-driven. Extensive ablations reveal that in low-data/small-model regimes, inductive biases improve sample efficiency. However, as data and model size scale, these benefits diminish or even reverse, while all-to-all attention remains critical for capturing LR interactions. Our model achieves state-of-the-art energy/force accuracy on molecular systems, as well as a number of physics-based evaluations (OMol25), while being competitive on materials (OMat24) and catalysts (OC20). Furthermore, it enables stable, long-timescale MD simulations that accurately recover experimental observables, including density and heat of vaporization predictions.
