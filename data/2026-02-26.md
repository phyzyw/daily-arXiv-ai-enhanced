### [1] [Towards single-shot coherent imaging via overlap-free ptychography](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.21361)
*Oliver Hoidn, Aashwin Mishra, Steven Henke, Albert Vong, Matthew Seaberg*

Main category: physics.optics

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种名为PtychoPINN的新框架，它结合了可微分的相干散射模型和泊松光子计数似然函数，实现了无需重叠的单次拍摄重建，并显著加速了传统的多次拍摄断层扫描。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现代同步辐射和X射线自由电子激光光源产生的数据速率远超图像重建速度，限制了实时反馈和实验控制。传统的断层扫描方法需要大量的重叠扫描，且计算速度慢，而直接的监督学习方法泛化能力差且需要大量训练数据。

Method: PtychoPINN将可微分的正向模型与泊松光子计数似然函数耦合，将真实空间重叠视为可调参数，而非硬性要求。该框架支持单次拍摄的无重叠重建，并利用坐标分组实现灵活的重叠控制。通过端到端的自监督训练，优化整个系统。

Result: 在合成基准测试中，PtychoPINN在低计数率下仍能保持准确的重建，单次拍摄重建的结构相似性 (SSIM) 达到 0.904，优于有重叠约束重建的 0.968。与相同骨干结构的监督模型相比，PtychoPINN仅使用 1024 张图像即可实现更高的 SSIM，并能泛化到未知的照明剖面。GPU 吞吐量约为传统 LSQ-ML 重建的 40 倍。

Conclusion: PtychoPINN 统一了单次曝光的菲涅尔断层扫描和重叠断层扫描，为现代光源提供了高效、高通量的成像支持，有望推动X射线纳米尺度成像技术的发展。

Abstract: Ptychographic imaging at synchrotron and XFEL sources requires dense overlapping scans, limiting throughput and increasing dose. Extending coherent diffractive imaging to overlap-free operation on extended samples remains an open problem. Here, we extend PtychoPINN (O. Hoidn \emph{et al.}, \emph{Scientific Reports} \textbf{13}, 22789, 2023) to deliver \emph{overlap-free, single-shot} reconstructions in a Fresnel coherent diffraction imaging (CDI) geometry while also accelerating conventional multi-shot ptychography. The framework couples a differentiable forward model of coherent scattering with a Poisson photon-counting likelihood; real-space overlap enters as a tunable parameter via coordinate-based grouping rather than a hard requirement. On synthetic benchmarks, reconstructions remain accurate at low counts ($\sim\!10^4$ photons/frame), and overlap-free single-shot reconstruction with an experimental probe reaches amplitude structural similarity (SSIM) 0.904, compared with 0.968 for overlap-constrained reconstruction. Against a data-saturated supervised model with the same backbone (16,384 training images), PtychoPINN achieves higher SSIM with only 1,024 images and generalizes to unseen illumination profiles. Per-graphics processing unit (GPU) throughput is approximately $40\times$ that of least-squares maximum-likelihood (LSQ-ML) reconstruction at matched $128\times128$ resolution. These results, validated on experimental data from the Advanced Photon Source and the Linac Coherent Light Source, unify single-exposure Fresnel CDI and overlapped ptychography within one framework, supporting dose-efficient, high-throughput imaging at modern light sources.

### [2] [Asymptotically Fast Clebsch-Gordan Tensor Products with Vector Spherical Harmonics](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.21466)
*YuQing Xie, Ameya Daigavane, Mit Kotak, Tess Smidt*

Main category: cs.LG

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种新的算法，能够以近最优的时间复杂度计算Clebsch-Gordan张量积 (CGTP)，显著加速了E(3)等变神经网络的计算。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有的加速CGTP的方法通常会降低模型的表达能力，而真正的算法改进却难以实现。之前的Gaunt张量积方法虽然有潜力，但并不完整。因此，需要一种能够提供真正渐近加速且完整的CGTP算法。

Method: 该算法通过推广快速傅里叶变换卷积，并引入张量球面谐函数，解决了Gaunt张量积方法中的反称问题。研究人员证明了张量谐函数的推广Gaunt公式，并发现只需要向量值信号即可恢复Gaunt张量积中缺失的相互作用。

Result: 新算法将CGTP的运行时间复杂度从朴素的O(L6)降低到O(L4log2L)，接近于O(L4)的下界。

Conclusion: 该算法为E(3)等变神经网络的扩展提供了重要的支持，使其能够处理更大规模的系统，并有望推动该领域的发展。

Abstract: $E(3)$-equivariant neural networks have proven to be effective in a wide range of 3D modeling tasks. A fundamental operation of such networks is the tensor product, which allows interaction between different feature types. Because this operation scales poorly, there has been considerable work towards accelerating this interaction. However, recently \citet{xieprice} have pointed out that most speedups come from a reduction in expressivity rather than true algorithmic improvements on computing Clebsch-Gordan tensor products. A modification of Gaunt tensor product \citep{gaunt} can give a true asymptotic speedup but is incomplete and misses many interactions. In this work, we provide the first complete algorithm which truly provides asymptotic benefits Clebsch-Gordan tensor products. For full CGTP, our algorithm brings runtime complexity from the naive $O(L^6)$ to $O(L^4\log^2 L)$, close to the lower bound of $O(L^4)$. We first show how generalizing fast Fourier based convolution naturally leads to the previously proposed Gaunt tensor product \citep{gaunt}. To remedy antisymmetry issues, we generalize from scalar signals to irrep valued signals, giving us tensor spherical harmonics. We prove a generalized Gaunt formula for the tensor harmonics. Finally, we show that we only need up to vector valued signals to recover the missing interactions of Gaunt tensor product.

### [3] [MBD-ML: Many-body dispersion from machine learning for molecules and materials](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.22086)
*Evgeny Moerman, Adil Kabylda, Almaz Khabibrakhmanov, Alexandre Tkatchenko*

Main category: physics.chem-ph

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种基于预训练消息传递神经网络的MBD-ML方法，可以直接从原子结构预测原子性质，从而简化了范德华相互作用的计算。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统的范德华相互作用方法需要繁琐的电子结构计算，限制了其在大规模模拟和机器学习中的应用。MBD方法虽然准确，但同样依赖电子结构计算。

Method: MBD-ML使用预训练的消息传递神经网络直接预测原子C6系数和极化率，并与libMBD无缝集成，实现MBD相互作用的快速计算，无需中间电子结构计算。

Result: MBD-ML能够直接计算MBD包含的总能量、力以及应力张量，显著简化了范德华相互作用的计算流程。

Conclusion: MBD-ML提供了一种实用且高效的工具，可以简化将最先进的范德华相互作用纳入任何电子结构代码、经验势和机器学习力场的过程，促进了大规模模拟和机器学习应用。

Abstract: Van der Waals (vdW) interactions are essential for describing molecules and materials, from drug design and catalysis to battery applications. These omnipresent interactions must also be accurately included in machine-learned force fields. The many-body dispersion (MBD) method stands out as one of the most accurate and transferable approaches to capture vdW interactions, requiring only atomic $C_6$ coefficients and polarizabilities as input. We present MBD-ML, a pretrained message passing neural network that predicts these atomic properties directly from atomic structures. Through seamless integration with libMBD, our method enables the immediate calculation of MBD-inclusive total energies, forces, and stress tensors. By eliminating the need for intermediate electronic structure calculations, MBD-ML offers a practical and streamlined tool that simplifies the incorporation of state-of-the-art vdW interactions into any electronic structure code, as well as empirical and machine-learned force fields.

</details>
