### [1] [Optimal Experimental Design for Reliable Learning of History-Dependent Constitutive Laws](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.12365)
*Kaushik Bhattacharya, Lianghao Cao, Andrew Stuart*

Main category: cond-mat.mtrl-sci

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种基于贝叶斯框架的优化实验设计方法，旨在提高对具有历史依赖性的本构模型的参数学习的可靠性，尤其是在实验预算有限的情况下。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统的本构模型参数识别依赖于实验数据，但有限的实验预算可能导致数据不足，参数估计不确定或不可靠。为了解决这个问题，需要一种方法来优化实验设计，以最大化信息获取并减少参数不确定性。

Method: 该框架通过定义设计效用（期望的参数不确定性降低或信息增益）来优化实验设计。作者引入了高斯近似和Fisher信息矩阵的替代近似，以降低计算成本，使其适用于复杂的材料测试和高维数据。通过模拟数据进行优化。

Result: 数值研究表明，优化的试样几何形状和加载路径可以产生显著改善参数可识别性的图像和力数据，尤其是在与记忆效应相关的参数方面，相比随机设计效果更好。

Conclusion: 该研究提供了一种实用的优化实验设计方法，可以有效地提高本构模型参数识别的可靠性，从而改进材料预测和决策。

Abstract: History-dependent constitutive models serve as macroscopic closures for the aggregated effects of micromechanics. Their parameters are typically learned from experimental data. With a limited experimental budget, eliciting the full range of responses needed to characterize the constitutive relation can be difficult. As a result, the data can be well explained by a range of parameter choices, leading to parameter estimates that are uncertain or unreliable. To address this issue, we propose a Bayesian optimal experimental design framework to quantify, interpret, and maximize the utility of experimental designs for reliable learning of history-dependent constitutive models. In this framework, the design utility is defined as the expected reduction in parametric uncertainty or the expected information gain. This enables in silico design optimization using simulated data and reduces the cost of physical experiments for reliable parameter identification.   We introduce two approximations that make this framework practical for advanced material testing with expensive forward models and high-dimensional data: (i) a Gaussian approximation of the expected information gain, and (ii) a surrogate approximation of the Fisher information matrix. The former enables efficient design optimization and interpretation, while the latter extends this approach to batched design optimization by amortizing the cost of repeated utility evaluations. Our numerical studies of uniaxial tests for viscoelastic solids show that optimized specimen geometries and loading paths yield image and force data that significantly improve parameter identifiability relative to random designs, especially for parameters associated with memory effects.

### [2] [Scaling Laws and Pathologies of Single-Layer PINNs: Network Width and PDE Nonlinearity](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.12556)
*Faris Chaudhry*

Main category: cs.LG

TL;DR: 本文研究了单层物理信息神经网络（PINNs）在非线性偏微分方程（PDE）中的扩展规律，发现其性能受限于优化问题而非网络表达能力，且非线性程度加剧了这种限制。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有研究缺乏对PINNs模型容量、问题复杂度和解精度之间关系的量化框架。本文旨在弥补理论表达能力与实际性能之间的差距，探究优化挑战在PINNs中的作用，特别是光谱偏差的影响。

Method: 通过对一系列经典非线性PDE（如Korteweg-de Vries、Sine-Gordon、Allen-Cahn）进行实验，系统地测量和分析了网络宽度和非线性程度的扩展指数，并采用可分离幂律模型进行拟合，以评估扩展行为。

Result: 实验结果表明，单层PINNs的解误差与网络宽度之间的关系未能达到理论近似边界，存在优化失败的现象。这种失败不仅是基线病理，而且随着非线性程度的增加而加剧。可分离幂律模型无法准确描述这种扩展行为，表明存在更复杂的非可分离关系。

Conclusion: 本文揭示了单层PINNs在优化方面的瓶颈，并提出了测量复杂扩展效应的方法。研究结果强调了光谱偏差在PINNs训练中的重要作用，为改进PINNs的优化算法和架构提供了新的方向。

Abstract: We establish empirical scaling laws for Single-Layer Physics-Informed Neural Networks on canonical nonlinear PDEs. We identify a dual optimization failure: (i) a baseline pathology, where the solution error fails to decrease with network width, even at fixed nonlinearity, falling short of theoretical approximation bounds, and (ii) a compounding pathology, where this failure is exacerbated by nonlinearity. We provide quantitative evidence that a simple separable power law is insufficient, and that the scaling behavior is governed by a more complex, non-separable relationship. This failure is consistent with the concept of spectral bias, where networks struggle to learn the high-frequency solution components that intensify with nonlinearity. We show that optimization, not approximation capacity, is the primary bottleneck, and propose a methodology to empirically measure these complex scaling effects.

### [3] [From Experiments to Expertise: Scientific Knowledge Consolidation for AI-Driven Computational Research](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.13191)
*Haonan Huang*

Main category: physics.comp-ph

TL;DR: 本文提出了QMatSuite平台，旨在解决现有AI驱动的计算科学中知识积累和利用不足的问题，通过知识记录、检索和反思机制，提升AI代理的科研能力。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有AI驱动的计算科学虽然在模拟执行方面取得了进展，但缺乏人类研究人员的经验积累和知识巩固能力，导致每次运行都如同独立事件，无法有效利用历史数据。

Method: QMatSuite平台通过三个核心支柱实现知识积累：一是抽象模拟复杂性，提供统一的API接口；二是确保可重复性研究，记录完整的溯源信息；三是提供持久的科学记忆系统，支持知识记录、检索和反思。

Result: 在六步量子力学模拟工作流程的基准测试中，知识积累使推理开销减少了67%，准确率从47%提高到与文献3%的偏差，并将成果成功迁移到不熟悉的材料，实现了1%的偏差和零管道故障。

Conclusion: QMatSuite平台证明了知识巩固对于AI驱动的计算科学至关重要，并表明专门的反思环节能够有效提升AI代理的效率和准确性，为AI在科研领域的应用提供了新的思路和工具。

Abstract: While large language models (LLMs) have transformed AI agents into proficient executors of computational materials science, performing a hundred simulations does not make a researcher. What distinguishes research from routine execution is the progressive accumulation of knowledge -- learning which approaches fail, recognizing patterns across systems, and applying understanding to new problems. However, the prevailing paradigm in AI-driven computational science treats each execution in isolation, largely discarding hard-won insights between runs. Here we present QMatSuite, an open-source platform closing this gap. Agents record findings with full provenance, retrieve knowledge before new calculations, and in dedicated reflection sessions correct erroneous findings and synthesize observations into cross-compound patterns. In benchmarks on a six-step quantum-mechanical simulation workflow, accumulated knowledge reduces reasoning overhead by 67% and improves accuracy from 47% to 3% deviation from literature -- and when transferred to an unfamiliar material, achieves 1% deviation with zero pipeline failures.

</details>
